Simple. Direct.

Our role — and our limits
TeraPlot is a marketplace platform only. We connect buyers and sellers — we do not participate in, verify, guarantee, or take responsibility for any land transaction. All deals are made directly between the buyer and seller. We strongly recommend verifying land documents independently through Rwanda's Land Authority (RLMA) before completing any transaction.
Common questions
Is it free to list a plot on TeraPlot?
+
Yes. Creating a basic listing is completely free. In the future we will offer promoted listings for sellers who want more visibility, but the core platform will always have a free tier.
How do I know a seller is legitimate?
+
Sellers with a ✓ Verified badge have submitted their national ID and land documents to our team for manual review. We also show each seller's rating and deal history. That said, we always recommend doing your own due diligence and verifying the title deed at RLMA before any payment.
Does TeraPlot take a commission on deals?
+
No. TeraPlot does not take any commission on land transactions. We are not involved in the deal at all — we simply introduce the buyer and seller to each other.
Which districts are covered?
+
Currently the platform focuses on Kigali (Kicukiro, Gasabo, Nyarugenge) and is expanding to all 30 districts of Rwanda. Sellers from any district can post listings today.
What documents should a seller upload?
+
We recommend uploading the title deed (Certificat d'enregistrement), the plot survey map, and any RLMA transfer documentation. Documents are stored securely and only shared with buyers you approve.
